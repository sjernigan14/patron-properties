## NEIGHBORHOOD OVERVIEW: SUMMERVILLE

**Historic Context:**
- Established late 1700s, listed on Georgia/National Registers of Historic Places (1980)
- Originally built around Augusta Arsenal (now Augusta University campus)
- Known as "The Hill" — hilltop location elevated above downtown

**Residential Character:**
- ~2,400 homes in neighborhood
- Mix of antebellum, Victorian, and modern estates
- Square lots with mature landscaping
- Pedestrian-friendly; walkable to dining, retail, recreation

**Proximity to Anchors:**
- Augusta National Golf Course (Masters Tournament home)
- Augusta Country Club (655 Milledge Rd)
- Augusta University Summerville Campus
- Morris Museum of Art
- Hickman Park (tennis, playgrounds, picnic)
- Lake Olmstead Park (~1 mile northeast)
- Downtown Augusta (~3 miles)

**Market Data (2024–2025):**
- **Median Home Price:** $310K (Dec 2024)
- **Average Sale Price:** $318K–$370K
- **Homes for Sale:** 24 (ranging $142K–$1.75M)
- **Days on Market:** ~44 days
- **Condos for Sale:** 2 (ranging $150K)

**Affluence Indicators:**
- Median/average prices align with affluent suburban Augusta
- Historic prestige (Presidents' homes, museums)
- Country club membership proximity
- Professional/managerial homeowner base

## DATA ACCESS ASSESSMENT

### What Works (Free):
✅ **Homes.com** — Best current listings, beds/baths, estimated value, past sales
✅ **Redfin** — MLS data, recent sales, market trends
✅ **Zillow** — Zestimate, rental comps, neighborhood data
✅ **Trulia** — Historical sales, owner contact tips
✅ **Spokeo / Rehold** — Owner names, sometimes contact info
✅ **LoopNet** — Commercial/investment property classification
✅ **Google Maps + Street View** — Neighborhood visual assessment
✅ **Richmond County Tax Assessor** (augustarichmondtaxassessor.com) — Assessed value, tax info

### What Doesn't Work (Blocked):
❌ **qPublic.schneidercorp.com** — Richmond County assessor GIS search (network egress blocked)
❌ **gismap.augustaga.gov** — Augusta GIS map (network egress blocked)

### What Would Improve Conversion (Commercial Data):
⚠️ **Worth $2K if:**
- Current phone/email validation (10–15% of prospects unreachable via free data)
- Confirmed occupancy status (owner vs. renter)
- Lifestyle/life-event triggers (new resident, empty nester, renovation permits)
- Income/wealth verification (filter top 30% by financial capacity)
- Neighborhood affinity clusters (find similar neighborhoods for scaling)

⚠️ **Not Worth $2K if:**
- Pilot conversion rate <10% (too much friction in sales process, not data quality)
- You're doing this for real estate investment (data accuracy isn't primary blocker)
- Team isn't ready to action 100+ qualified leads/month

- **Year Built Accuracy:** Estimated for pre-1950 homes; some off by 10–20 years
